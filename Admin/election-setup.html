🔒 Voting Rules & Restrictions

🏛️
General Seats
All students (UG + PG, any department, any gender) can vote for all general college union seats.
🏫
Department Representatives
UG students vote only UG Dept Rep. PG students vote only PG Dept Rep. Cross-level voting blocked.
👩
Lady Representative
Only female students can apply for and vote for Lady Representative seats (2 selections).
🔁
Double Vote Prevention
Once a student casts their vote, the system permanently locks their ballot to prevent re-voting.
